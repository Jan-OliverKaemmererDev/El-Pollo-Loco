El Pollo Loco

How to Play

Navigate Pepe through the desert to defeat the evil chicken boss!

  • 🏃 Move through the level using arrow keys
  • 🦅 Jump on enemies to squash them
  • 🍾 Collect bottles dropped by defeated enemies
  • 🎯 Throw bottles at the Endboss to defeat him
  • ❤️ Watch your health bar — avoid getting hit!

Reach the end of the level and defeat the boss chicken to win!

Keyboard Controls

← Move Left
→ Move Right
↑ Jump
SPACE Jump
D Throw Bottle

The Story

In the scorching deserts of Mexico, Pepe — a brave adventurer — discovers that the local chicken population has gone completely loco!

Led by a giant, fearsome Boss Chicken, the poultry army is terrorizing the countryside! Armed only with his wits and an unlimited supply of salsa bottles, Pepe must fight his way through hordes of crazy chickens.

Can you help Pepe defeat El Pollo Loco and restore peace to the desert? 🌵🐔
